Cybersecurity: Cybersecurity Challenges That Organizations Are Facing

By Mohammed Elansary 3 Min Read

Cybersecurity is challenged by rapidly emerging threats, employees, lack of skilled people, complex IT environment, regulatory compliance, advanced persistent threats, and third party. Organizations should therefore employ advanced technologies, recruit and train their personnel well, and set up good policies and procedures in terms of security from ever changing cyber threats.

Cybersecurity: Protecting the Future

By NCST IT Club 5 Min Read

Cybersecurity has become a top priority at NCST. With our IT Club leading the way, students are learning how to stay safe online and protect sensitive information. NCST is organizing regular workshops to help students stay informed about the latest trends in cyber threats and how to defend against them. Our students have also been participating in various online competitions to hone their skills. This initiative has been instrumental in building a strong cybersecurity culture on campus.
